Find the Right Size

Height is the primary factor for gi sizing. Weight is used as a secondary check — if they suggest different sizes, we'll recommend the larger one and note it.

Gi sizes vary by brand. This is a general guide — always check the brand's specific size chart before buying. Kids' sizing (M000–M4) follows the IBJJF standard; individual manufacturers may differ slightly.
