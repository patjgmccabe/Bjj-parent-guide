For Parents on the Sidelines

BJJ Parent Guide is a free resource for parents watching from the sidelines. Every BJJ term explained. Every tournament rule decoded. The gear guide you wish someone had handed you on day one.

You don't need to train to understand what your kid is learning — you just need someone to translate it.

Start with our BJJ Glossary to decode what the coach is saying, check the Belt Guide to understand your child's progress, and read the Tournament Guide before your first competition day. If you're buying a gi, our Gi Size Finder takes the guesswork out of sizing.
